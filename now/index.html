This is my now page, where you can find what I'm currently up to.

This now page used to just display the same stuff with the what I'm reading/listening to changing occasionally. Instead, I'm going to update it each month with what I'm focusing on (sometimes updating throughout the month).

You can see previous monthly updates in the then section.

Work

After about a month of being unemployed, I accepted a job offer and started at the end of November. Finding a job went much quicker than I anticipated.

Covid

After about two and a half years of avoiding it (somehow) someone in my household tested positive for covid. It took a few weeks to have negative tests for everyone, but we are now covid-free. Only about half of us got it and it didn't seem to be too bad of a strain.

Birds

I set up a window bird feeder over the summer for viewing birds as they came to eat. Unfortunately they never went over to it, but I left it up and now that winter is approaching, we've had quite a few visitors!

These are the birds I saw this month:

  • Northern Flicker
  • House Finch (male, which I've never seen before)

I also saw a hawk the closest I've seen one. It swooped down and got something off the ground and went up to a tree. I was in the car so it was only for a moment, but it was really cool to see.
