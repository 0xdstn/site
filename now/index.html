Birds

We took a family vacation to the Oregon coast, so I saw a lot of sea birds this month.

We went to the Wildlife Safari in Winston, OR and the Oregon Zoo, so I was able to see a lot of various birds at those places. Those birds haven't been listed below. The birds listed are ones that I saw around outside of zoos.

These are the birds I saw this month:

  • Brewer's Blackbird
  • Double-crested Cormorant
  • Rock Pigeon
  • Glaucous-winged Gull
  • Herring Gull
  • Acorn Woodpecker
  • Great Blue Heron
