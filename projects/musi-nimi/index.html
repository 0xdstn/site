💻 musi nimi

A hosted version of Wordle, but using 4 letter Toki Pona words. There are 69 4 letter words in Toki Pona, more than any other word length, so those are used for the dictionary of possible words.

I did not build this from scratch. I copied the actual source files from Wordle and modified them. I take no credit for creating this game, I just wanted a Toki Pona version for myself to play, and I figured I'd share it for others to play as well.

UI translations to toki pona were provided by @tepo@toki.social.

Live version: https://tilde.town/~dustin/wordle-toki/
