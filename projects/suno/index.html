suno

A "morning page" writing tool where the posts disappear the next day.

It stores your day's writing in local storage, and erases it the next day. The intent with this is for you to be able to write without worrying about the contents, as they will be gone within 24 hours.

It is set up as a PWA, so you can also install it to your android device and even use it offline.

  • Source code: https://github.com/0xdstn/suno/
  • Live version: http://tilde.town/~dustin/suno/
