🥣 Apple Cinnamon Oats

  1. Ingredients
  2. Directions
  3. Suggested Alterations

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp plant-based milk
  • 1/2 an apple
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions

  1. Chop up the apple into 1/2 inch pieces
  2. In a bowl, combine spices
  3. In a small pot, combine the oats and water
  4. Cook on medium until boiled (~7 minutes), constantly stirring
  5. Stir in the plant-based milk and cook for another minute or so
  6. Add the oats to the bowl and mix well
  7. Top with apples and sprinkle on a little more cinnamon

Suggested Alterations

This recipe is not very sweet. If you like things sweeter you could add 1 tsp of brown sugar to the bowl with the other spices.
