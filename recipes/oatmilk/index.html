🥣 Oatmilk

  1. Ingredients
  2. Directions
  3. Notes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Combine ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend for ~30 seconds
  3. Strain through a nut milk bag

Notes

  • I use this nut milk bag
  • The pulp left in the bag can be saved and used to add nutrients to and thicken up smoothies
