🥣 Peanut butter oats

  1. Ingredients
  2. Directions

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp plant-based milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana

Directions

  1. In a small pot, combine the oats and water
  2. Cook on medium until boiled (~7 minutes), constantly stirring
  3. Stir in the plant-based milk and cook for another minute or so
  4. Add the oats to a bowl
  5. Stir in the peanut butter and honey
  6. Slice the banana and place on top of the oats
