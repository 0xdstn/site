Pumpkin overnight oats

  1. Ingredients
  2. Directions
  3. Suggested Alterations

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1/2 cup plant-based milk
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp powdered ginger
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions

  1. In a jar or container, combine the all ingredients
  2. Mix well
  3. Place in fridge overnight

Suggested Alterations

This recipe yeilds fairly thick oats. If you prefer them to be less thick, you could increase the amount of milk to 3/4 cup.
