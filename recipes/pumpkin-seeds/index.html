Roasted Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Spices

Spice ideas

  • Salt & pepper
  • Pumpkin pie spice
  • Garlic powder

Directions

  1. Remove pumpkin seeds from pumpkin, rinse
  2. Spread out somewhere to dry out
  3. Pre-heat oven to 200 °
  4. In a bowl combine seeds, oil, and spices
  5. Spread seeds on a baking sheet
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
  7. Turn the heat up to 325°
  8. Bake for 5 minutes or until crisp (they will start popping when about done)
