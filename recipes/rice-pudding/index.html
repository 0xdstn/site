🥣 Rice Pudding

  1. Description
  2. Ingredients
  3. Directions

Description

This recipe is taken from this recipe by James Chip. The only difference is I converted the measurements and added a cook time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pudding rice
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 cups oat milk
  • Nutmeg

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F
  2. Mix the rice, sugar, and milk in an oven safe dish.
  3. Sprinkle nutmeg over the top
  4. Bake in oven until the liquid is absorbed, about 1 hr 40 min.
