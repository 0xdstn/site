🥣 Winter oats

  1. Description
  2. Ingredients
  3. Directions
  4. Suggested Alterations

Description

This recipe is a slightly modified version of this recipe by ritualdust.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp plant-based milk
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/3 cup whole pecans
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions

  1. In a small pot, combine the oats and water
  2. Cook on medium until boiled (~7 minutes), constantly stirring
  3. Stir in the plant-based milk and cook for another minute or so
  4. In a bowl, combine spices
  5. Add the oats to the bowl and mix well
  6. Top with cranberries and pecans

Suggested Alterations

Originally this recipe had 1 tsp of brown sugar, but no longer include it. If you like things sweeter you could add that.
