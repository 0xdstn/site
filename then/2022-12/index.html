December 2022

Birds

We have a new visitor that's taken over our bird feeder - Dark-eyed Junkos. It seems they may have taken over, as the house finches haven't been stopping by anymore, or at least not as often.

We also had two Northern Flickers visit our hanging bird feeder!

These are the birds I saw this month:

  • Dark-eyed Junko
  • House Finch
  • Northern Flicker

Reading

I decided Goodreads isn't the best book management solution for me personally, so I created my own tool. You can see it and more about it here:

  • Project post: Books
  • Source code: https://github.com/0xdstn/books/
  • Live version: https://tilde.town/~dustin/books/

I actually didn't finish any books in December. I went into the month at 30 books, which was my goal. I started a couple of books, but didn't manage to get all the way through them.

Moving forward I want to improve my relationship to reading. Aside from no longer using goodreads, I am also not going to set a yearly goal, and be more relaxed about what and when I read.
