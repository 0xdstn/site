📝 100 Days to Offload

This is my first post in the 100 days to offload challenge! I have no idea what I am going to write about, but I'm looking forward to writing more and seeing what others write as well!

I was inspired to start this because 82mhz posted that he would be doing it starting September 1, 2023 and asked if others wanted to join. There are a few of us now, which is great!

You can find a list of participants on 82mhz's first post. There is also an IRC channel set up by Matto. The channel is #phloggersgarage on Libera.chat.

One thing I might do to help myself have something to write about is start doing week notes on Sundays, if I have some things to post an update on. I had never heard of this, but just came across it on this post from Noisy Deadlines.

🍂 Autumn

I am really looking forward to autumn. It's my favorite season, and cooler weather has already started rolling in. We've had quite a few rainy and cool days. There was even some beautiful fog with a (nearly) full moon last night!

I'm sitting here in my kitchen with a nice cup of coffee, cool air blowing in from an open window, writing this, and all feels great. 💙

I'm trying to slow down. This is something I've tried many times, but I always just end up doing too much again, burning out, and feeling stressed all the time. I want a more relaxed, intentional, and slow paced kind of life. Maybe writing things down here will help with that? I guess we'll see.
