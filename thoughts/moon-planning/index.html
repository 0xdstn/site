💭 Moon Planning

2024.01.02

I last wrote in my 2024 plans post that I was thinking of focusing on something each quarter (buddhism, witchcraft, etc). I've thought about it since and I have a new plan - set a focus for each moon cycle. This will allow me to switch it up more often (there are 12 full cycles this year). The new moon is a time for planning, and you can harness the moon's energy by setting intentions during a new moon.

This also opens it up so I have check in points (first quarter, full, and last quarter), where I can assess how I'm doing and make sure I'm staying on track.

When each new moon comes around I will reflect on the previous cycle, and determine the focus for the next cycle.

The first full moon is on January 11th, so I am going to spend this time planning exactly how these rituals will look, and determine what I want my first cycle's focus to be.

In addition to setting a focus and checking in on my progress of it on each moon phase, I am going to try out doing a personal reflection with every phase, to give myself a scheduled time to reflect on how things are going in general and make changes.

These are the new moons this year:

01/11, 02/09, 03/10, 04/08, 05/07, 06/06, 07/05, 08/04, 09/02, 10/02, 11/01, 11/30, 12/30
