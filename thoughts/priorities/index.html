💭 Priorities

2024.01.17

This probably won't make much sense to others, but I have decided for personal reasons to stop writing on this site and to end my #100DaysToOffload challenge. I am finding that sharing here is causing a severe lack in sharing elsewhere in my life, and it is problematic for my relationships.

I'm grateful for everyone that has read what I've posted and encouraged me along the way.

I will leave the posts in the writing, thoughts, and changelog sections in place so they don't become dead links, but I will be hiding those sections from my navigation. I will still be adding posts to projects, wiki, art, recipes, and toki pona.

Again, I know this may not make sense to everyone, but I know that this is best for myself.

See you space cowboy...
