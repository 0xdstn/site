💭 Tales From the Crypt

2023.09.18

I've been aware of Tales From the Crypt for a long time, but have never actually read or watched any of it's media.

I was researching it last night and found that all of the seasons of the show, the radio show, and the children's show are on archive.org!

  • Tales From the Crypt seasons 1-2
  • Tales From the Crypt season 3
  • Tales From the Crypt seasons 4-5
  • Tales From the Crypt seasons 6-7
  • Tales From the Cryptkeeper full series
  • Tales From The Crypt Radio Series

It also looks like Hoopla has many (all? I'm a little unsure here) of the comics.

The only thing I don't have access to without paying are the 3 movies, but I'm looking forward to diving into all of this!
