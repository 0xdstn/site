💭 Toki Pona Section

2023.12.08

Since I am trying to take my learning toki pona seriously, I've created a new section on my site for it!

I will only be writing in toki pona on the pages of that section. I am also using the linja pona font to display toki pona in sitelen pona. This would probably look weird in a RSS feed because sometimes you use hyphens and brackets and things to display compound glyphs, so I've excluded it from my RSS feed. At some point I may create a separate toki pona feed that cleans it up a bit, for those that want to follow it. I also didn't want to bog down my RSS feed with a language people may not know.

I have only added one page so far, which is where I will keep my toki pona haiku: sitelen musi lili.

I am still learning toki pona, so if anyone reading this has some suggestions on my writing, please reach out and let me know so I can learn from it.
