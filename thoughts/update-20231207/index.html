Toki Pona

I've decided to start attempting to learn toki pona again. I am trying out Anki this time. It seems to be going well actually, I think I've learned a couple new words and gotten more confident on the ones that were less automatic for me. I already know most toki pona words, but I have some that are really difficult to get to stick for some reason. Hoping this will result in me knowing all of them. I then will work on reading things such as lipu tenpo. I'd also like to write more in toki pona, either in my journal or on here. I did write a haiku the other day:

telo sewi o
telo mute lon ale
kasi li pona

This roughly translates to:

rain!
a lot of water is on everything
the plant is happy
