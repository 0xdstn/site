Moon Manifesting

The secret to manifesting is that it comes from the heart and that you feel as though it's already happened.

Note what you want, not what you don't want.

You cannot override the law of physics or someone else's free will.

Limited to what we believe is possible.

Pay attention to signs from the universe - Synchronicity

Don't wish for things, wish for the feelings they evoke.

Envy blocks gratitude and being grateful is required to manifest.

The future is created in the present.

Meditation is crucial. You're like a radio. Your wishes are radio waves, letting the universe know what you want. When you meditate, it boosts your signal.

Relaxed + stress free = better manifesting

It's not enough to make wishes then continue living a chaotic life.

Get clear on what you want, but don't get too attached to your dreams.

The universe may decide on something better. Trust.

New Moon Ritual

  1. Take a moment to feel gratitude for all you have
  2. Write down your plans / wishes / what you want
  3. Read through each and **visualize** them as already true. Really feel it
  4. Write down affirmations to back them up
  5. Go through each and think about how you intend to make it happen. Write them down.
  6. Meditate, release your wishes into the universe
  7. Release your attachment
  8. Carry on
