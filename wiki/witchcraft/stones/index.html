Gemstones

Carrying around or wearing specific gemstones can affect you in different ways. These are the ones I own.

  • Jade: Self acceptance, inner calm, creativity
  • Rose Quartz: Love
  • Jasper: Grounding and centering, luck and good fortune
  • Tiger's Eye: Self confidence, courage, protection
  • Amethyst: Calm, balance
  • Obsidian: Grounding, protection
  • Lapis Lazuli: Confusion, emotional baggage
  • Clear Quartz: Connection to all four elements

Hagstones

Hagstones are stones found by the ocean with holes in them that you can see through. They are supposed to let positive energy through, but negative energy doesn't fit through the holes, and are used for protection.
