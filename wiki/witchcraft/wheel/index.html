📘 Wheel of the Year

  1. Notes
  2. Sabbats

Notes

The Gods and Goddesses can be recognized, while not worshiped, on a secular level. You can develop an intuitively based wheel of the year practice that is genuine to your unique path.

Sabbats

  • Imbolc February 1
  • Ostara (Spring Equinox) March 19-22
  • Beltane May 1
  • Litha (Summer Solstice) June 19-23
  • Lammas August 1
  • Mabon (Autumn Equinox) September 21-24
  • Samhain November 1
  • Yule (Winter Solstice) December 20-23
